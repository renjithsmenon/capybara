Features Kids Love

Make reading fun and rewarding with a simple library built for young readers.

▣

My Library

Keep all your favourite books organized in one beautiful library. Browse covers in a visual grid, filter by read, unread, or favourites, and find any book instantly.

▣

Scan & Add

Point your camera at the ISBN barcode on the back of any book. The app looks it up automatically and fills in the title, author, cover, and more. You can also type an ISBN or add books manually.

♛

Fun Milestones

Earn reading levels from Bookworm to Hero as you read more books. Track monthly streaks, unlock achievement badges, and watch your progress bar fill up toward the next milestone.

▥

Reading Stats

A fun dashboard showing total books, books read, favourite types, recent activity, and milestone badges — all private, all stored on your device, with no tracking or analytics.
