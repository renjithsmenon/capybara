About Capybara Library

Helping kids fall in love with reading.

Our Story

Capybara Kids Library was designed by Dhyuthi R Menon — a young reader who believes every child deserves the joy of building their own book collection.

We built a safe, fun and simple app to help kids discover, collect and enjoy physical books while making it easy for families to track reading progress together.
